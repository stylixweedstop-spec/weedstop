Clarity Points are WeedStop's in-app currency earned through daily quizzes (1-3 pts per correct answer), reading articles (variable XP), playing games, and daily check-ins. Use points to unlock premium games (500 pts), customize themes (5 pts each), or restore chat access after violations (50 pts). Daily cap prevents farming, and you can watch ads for 10 free points when below 250.
WeedStop uses multi-layer verification to ensure authentic progress: (1) Time integrity checking detects clock manipulation, (2) Article comprehension quizzes verify actual reading, (3) Reading time and scroll tracking prevent gaming, (4) Game achievement verification with anti-cheat flags. Violations result in progressive penalties to protect the integrity of achievements and leaderboards.
WeedStop uses Mistral AI (mistral-small-latest model) trained as an expert recovery coach. The AI maintains conversation memory (last 7 messages), automatically responds in your preferred language, and has access to your full stats (sober days, money saved, relapse history) for personalized coaching. It provides empathetic but authoritative guidance for managing cravings and building healthy habits.
WeedStop uses a progressive, compassionate approach: First slip = gentle penalty (capped at 20 joints), AI coach offers support. Multiple relapses = increasing penalties. After 3rd relapse, you get the option (not requirement) to reset data and start fresh. This system recognizes that recovery isn't always linear while maintaining accountability.
